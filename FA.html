Frequently Asked Questions

Services & Process

What interior design services do you offer?
We offer a comprehensive range of services including residential design, commercial spaces, color consultation, furniture selection, space planning, 3D visualization, and project management. Each project is tailored to your specific needs and vision.
How does the design process work?
Our process begins with an initial consultation to understand your vision and requirements. We then develop a concept, create detailed designs, present them for your feedback, and once approved, we assist with implementation and sourcing of materials and furnishings.
Do you work with contractors and architects?
Yes, we collaborate with a network of trusted contractors, architects, and craftsmen to ensure your project is executed seamlessly. We can manage these relationships on your behalf or work with your existing team.

Pricing & Packages

What are your pricing structures?
We offer flexible pricing options including flat fee packages, hourly rates, and percentage-based pricing for larger projects. During our initial consultation, we'll recommend the best approach for your specific needs and provide a detailed estimate.
Do you offer free consultations?
Yes, we offer a complimentary 30-minute initial consultation either virtually or in-person to discuss your project, understand your needs, and determine if we're a good fit for your vision.
What is included in your design packages?
Our packages typically include concept development, floor plans, color schemes, material selection, furniture recommendations, lighting plans, and access to trade discounts. Higher-tier packages include 3D renderings and project management services.

Timeline & Scheduling

How long does a typical project take?
Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A single room refresh may take 2-4 weeks, while a full home renovation can take 3-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase and keep you updated throughout the process.
How far in advance should I book your services?
We recommend booking 4-8 weeks in advance, especially for larger projects. However, we sometimes have availability for smaller consultations with shorter notice. Feel free to contact us to check our current availability.

Style & Customization

Can you work with my existing furniture?
Absolutely! We love incorporating cherished pieces into new designs. During our consultation, we'll assess your existing furniture and recommend how to best integrate it with new elements for a cohesive look.
What if I'm not sure about my style preferences?
Many clients come to us unsure of their style preferences. We have a structured process to help you discover your design aesthetic through questionnaires, inspiration boards, and samples. We'll guide you toward a style that reflects your personality and meets your functional needs.

Still have questions?

Get in touch with our team for personalized assistance with your interior design project.
